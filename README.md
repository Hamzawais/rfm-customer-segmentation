# 📊 RFM Customer Segmentation

An end-to-end customer analytics project that applies Recency, Frequency, and Monetary (RFM) analysis to segment customers, identify high-value customer groups, and support data-driven marketing strategies. The project combines exploratory data analysis, feature engineering, machine learning, and business intelligence techniques to deliver actionable customer insights.

---

# 📖 Project Overview

Customer segmentation is a key component of customer relationship management. By analyzing how recently customers purchased, how frequently they buy, and how much they spend, businesses can better understand customer behavior and design personalized marketing campaigns.

This project implements a complete RFM analysis pipeline, customer segmentation workflow, and predictive analytics models to classify valuable customers and support business decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Calculate RFM metrics.
- Perform customer segmentation.
- Engineer predictive features.
- Train multiple machine learning models.
- Identify high-value customer groups.
- Support targeted marketing campaigns.
- Generate business-ready customer insights.

---

# 💼 Business Problem

Every customer contributes differently to business revenue. Treating every customer the same results in inefficient marketing and lower customer retention.

RFM analysis helps organizations:

- Identify loyal customers.
- Detect customers at risk of leaving.
- Personalize marketing campaigns.
- Improve customer retention.
- Increase Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).

---

# 📂 Dataset

The project utilizes transactional customer data to calculate Recency, Frequency, and Monetary metrics.

The datasets include customer transaction history and aggregated RFM tables used for segmentation and predictive analytics.

> **Note:** Sample datasets are included for educational purposes. Sensitive customer information should always be anonymized.

# 🧩 Customer Segmentation

Customers are segmented using RFM analysis based on:

- **Recency** – How recently a customer made a purchase.
- **Frequency** – How often a customer makes purchases.
- **Monetary** – How much a customer spends.

These metrics help businesses prioritize customer engagement strategies and identify valuable customer groups.

---

# 🤖 Machine Learning

The project evaluates multiple classification algorithms, including:

- Logistic Regression
- Random Forest
- Gradient Boosting
- K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)
- Support Vector Machine (SVM)

Hyperparameter tuning and model evaluation are performed to compare predictive performance and identify the most effective approach.

# 🚀 Getting Started


